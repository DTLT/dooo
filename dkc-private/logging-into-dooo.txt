====== Logging into umw.domains ======
  - **Login** to **LastPass**, which is the Google Chrome extension in the upper right hand corner of the browser, next to three bars Chrome menu. To login, the username is **info@dkc.umwdtlt.org** and the password is ___. 
  - Once logged into LastPass, the asterisk square will turn from grey to red. 
  - Next, **login** to **umw.domains** as an //admin// by going to **umw.domains/login**.
  - Once there, you will see a the standard WordPress login page. 
  - To login to **WordPress** for umw.domains, click on the small asterisk on the right hand sign of the username bar. You will a drop down menu from different usernames to choose from. Pick **dtlt**.
  - The password will be generated automatically through LastPass, and then you can click **Log In**.
  - Now you are logged in! You should see cPanel along with the WordPress Admin bar at the very top of the screen.
