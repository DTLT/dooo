====== Resend Verification Email ======

Once logged into Domain of One's Own follow the instructions for "**Switching Users**". Switch to the appropriate student account that needs a new Verification Email.

1. Once switched to the user who needs the verification email, a colorized pop-up will appear at the top of the screen.
{{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3840/15208043908_f03f517e03_b.jpg}}

2. Click on "**Resend Verification Email**."

3. A new email has been sent to the user. Be sure to notify the user that the email maybe sent to their "**junk folder**" in their school email account.
