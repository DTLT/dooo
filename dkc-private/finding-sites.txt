======Finding Sites======
If the student doesn't know their username, but can tell you what class blog they previously used, you can find them through the class sites.
  - **Login** to UMW Blogs using LastPass. Use the second **dtlt** option. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3849/15216164628_6d0e785410_o.png}} {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2944/15399580271_f52393113b_o.png}}
  - Hover over the **My Sites** menu, which is on the left-hand side of the WordPress Admin bar.{{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3905/15379734066_82a7113c8f_o.png}}
  - Hover over the **Network Admin**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2949/15379734086_54355eb8fa_o.png}}
  - Click on **Dashboard**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3917/15216246007_f614a781f0_o.png}}
  - Hover over the **sites** menu.{{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2949/15379900136_b1ab123079_o.png}}
  - Click on **All Sites** {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3874/15216408847_b1ab123079_o.png}}
  - **Search** for their respective site name. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3909/15216265150_8098e900d0_c.jpg}}
