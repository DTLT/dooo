======Increasing Quotas======
  - **Login** to **UMW Domains** using **LastPass**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2947/15379387506_159b5af3bb_o.png}}
  - In the search bar on the left side of the screen, search **users**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3907/15215755500_334c533930_o.png}
  - Click on **List all users**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2941/15215819568_4aa90bcd23_o.png}}
  - Search for the user by using their username in the search bar in the middle of the screen. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3925/15215662259_e7a71b6fdc_o.png}}
  - After you have found that user, do some background research to find out why they are over their quota. 
    - To do this click on **cPanel**. {{Picture pointing to cPanel}}
  - Find the username and click the **Plus** button to open a menu. {{photo pointing to the plus button}}
  - Click on **Change Quota**. {{photo pointing to change quota}}
  -Add Quotas in **250 MB** increments. {{picture of the increment increase}}
