====== Managing Comments ======

Click on the comments tab on the left hand side to open the comments management area.

{{https://farm6.staticflickr.com/5611/15398688380_53ae9d7895_o_d.png}}

Then you will be directed to a screen similar to this, from here you have many different options available to manage your comments. It is highly recommended that you download the Akismet plugin and sign up for a free account because that will greatly reduce the number of spam comments.

{{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3934/15398181768_53dbe6a551_d.jpg}}
