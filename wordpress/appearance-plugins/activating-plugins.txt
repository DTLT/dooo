#Activating Plugins

1) Log into your account\\
2) Select the plugins menu from the left side bar\\

{{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3948/15371078817_181a3361f9_o_d.png}}\\

3) Click activate under the individual plugins

{{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3942/15533379196_5a15105a76_d.jpg}}

**Note: Some plugins may require additional activation steps.**
