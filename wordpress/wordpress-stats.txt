======Wordpress Statistics======
Wordpress Statistics are great if the user is looking to view the traffic that comes to their website. There are many plugins that will help with that. [[https://premium.wpmudev.org/project/install-and-configure-a-plugin/ | You can watch how to install and configure a plugin over here.]]

**Jetpack** is an easy way to add your statistics straight to your dashboard menu. 
  - After logging in and you are on your dashboard:
  - Hover over the **plugins** menu. 
    - Click on **Add New**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3931/15233685320_cba57efee0_o.png}}
  - **Install** the plugin **Jetpack** (Should be one of the featured plugins) {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2945/15233829327_d753939d19_o.png}}
  - Confirm the installation. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3929/15233814117_e04288ffc8_o.png}}
  - Click on **Activate**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2946/15420360575_e0b66052ec_c.jpg}}
  - Click on **Settings**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3928/15233685050_dcdfed2ceb_o.png}}
  - Click on **Connect to Get Started**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3931/15420360475_ece3a7634b_c.jpg}}
  - Connect your **Wordpress Account**. Its the **email** you used to sign up for the domain.
  -  You're all set! {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2942/15233611249_b35299c686_c.jpg}}
