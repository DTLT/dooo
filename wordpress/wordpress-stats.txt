======Wordpress Statistics======
Wordpress Statistics is great if the user is looking to view the traffic that comes to their website. There are many plugins that will help with that. [[https://premium.wpmudev.org/project/install-and-configure-a-plugin/ | You can watch how to install and configure a plugin over here.]]

**Jetpack** is an easy way to add your statistics straight to your dashboard menu. 
  - First you must log-in and go to your dashboard.
  - Hover over the **plugins** menu. 
    - Click on **Add New**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3931/15233685320_cba57efee0_o.png}}
  - **Install** the plugin **Jetpack** (Should be one of the featured plugins) {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2945/15233829327_d753939d19_o.png}}
  - Confirm the installation. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3929/15233814117_e04288ffc8_o.png}}
  - Click on **Activate**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2946/15420360575_e0b66052ec_c.jpg}}
  - Click on **Settings**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3928/15233685050_dcdfed2ceb_o.png}}
  - Click on **Connect to Get Started**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3931/15420360475_ece3a7634b_c.jpg}}
  - Connect your **Wordpress Account**. It's the **email** you used to sign up for the domain. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2946/15420021782_2b3ac6ce09_b.jpg}}
  -  You're all set! {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2942/15233611249_b35299c686_c.jpg}}

To integrate **Google Analytics** account into your blog:
  - Follow steps 1 and 2 above.
  - Search for **Google Analytics**. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2941/15234220937_e7d4f8d5dd_o.png}}
  - Install the plugin. {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2948/15234220237_e457776442_o.png}}
  - Click on **Activate**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3930/15234172498_89efa5b74b_c.jpg}}
  - Click on **Settings**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3929/15234014619_f5fd85be96_o.png}}
  - Click on **Authorize Plugin**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3928/15397724936_01aed80a05_b.jpg}}
  - Click on **Get Access Code**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3930/15397722926_18b60d597a_b.jpg}}
  - Click **Accept** {{https://farm3.staticflickr.com/2941/15420761385_f679b71f9f_o.png}}
  - Either sign in with your **Google** account or make a new one. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3928/15234221747_bfd64efe45_o.png}}
  - After signing in or creating account you will see the **code** to copy and paste (This one is blurred out). {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3931/15420783735_bc9d74b9a9_o.png}}
  - **Paste** the code into the window. 
  - Click **Save Access Code**. {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3929/15234107240_97ce93fd7f_b.jpg}}
  - Your Statistics should now be on your **Dashboard**! {{https://farm4.staticflickr.com/3927/15420430252_cd04f6447b_o.png}}
