====== Important Notice Regarding Your Domain Name(s) ======

If you receive an email with the subject line, “Important Notice Regarding Your Domain Name(s)” from support@umwdomains.com, do not be alarmed. It is not spam. It is an annual email sent out to users participating in Domain of One's Own. You do not have to reply to it. Just ignore it.
