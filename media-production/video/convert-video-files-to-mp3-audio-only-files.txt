===== Convert Video Files to MP3 (audio only) Files =====

==== Ingrediants ====

1 - Computer (Mac for this tutorial)

1 - Evom Program  (Mac only - Available at http://thelittleappfactory.com/evom/).

1 - MP4 video file you wish to convert to MP3

=== Directions ===

  - Open Evom (Available at http://thelittleappfactory.com/evom/)
  - Drag MP4 file (usually located in your movies folder) into Evom program.
  - When prompted for a location, choose something like your Music folder.
  - In the Format tab select MP3
  - Click on your quality of video you want for your MP3.
  - Then click Convert button
  - To find your MP3 clip, go to Music folder. Double click the MP3 clip and it will open in QuickTime.
