====== Creating a Camera Archive in Final Cut Pro X ======

===== Ingredients =====

1 - Computer (Mac)

1 - Video Camera

1 - Final Cut Pro X Software

===== Directions =====

  - Insert the SD Card from the video camera and insert it into the computer's SD Card Reader.
  - Open Final Cut Pro X to the project you would like.
  - Click **Import Media** (The down arrow on the left).
  - Click **Create Archive** (Lower left hand corner).
  - Navigate to where you would like to save the video files.
  - Name the archive and click **Create**.
  - Once the circle by the SD Card name has filled completely, you may remove the SD Card.
  - To import media from your archive, click **Import Media**, navigate to where you saved the archive, and import the media just as if it was a physical SD Card.
