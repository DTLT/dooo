====== Creating a Camera Archive in Final Cut Pro X ======

===== Ingredients =====

1 - Computer (Mac)

1 - Video Camera

1 - Final Cut Pro X Software

===== Directions =====

  - Open Final Cut Pro X
  - Insert the SD Card from the video camera and insert it into the computer's SD Card Reader. Alternately you can connect the USB cable from your camcorder to your computer.
  - If it doesn't open automatically, click the **Import Media** button (The down arrow icon on the left side of the toolbar).
  - Select the appropriate camera icon and click **Create Archive...** in the lower left corner.
  - Navigate to where you would like to save the archived video files.
  - Name the archive and click **Create**.
  - Once the circle by the camera name has filled completely, you may remove (eject) the SD Card or disconnect the USB cable.
  - To import media from your archive, click **Import Media**, navigate to where you saved the archive, and import the media just as if your camera was physically connected.
  - **NOTE** - You can read and import from a Camera Archive in either Final Cut Pro X or iMovie.
