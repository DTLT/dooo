====== Download YouTube Videos with 4K Downloader ======

===== Ingredients =====

1 - Computer (Mac or PC)

1 - Browser

1 - 4K Video Downloader Software

===== Directions =====

**NOTE** In order to download videos at full 4K resolution, the software requires the paid version which can be purchased at: [[http://www.4kdownload.com]]

  - Navigate your browser to the [[http://www.youtube.com|YouTube]] video you could like to download.
  - Copy the video URL (either by using the URL bar or the Share button).
  - In the 4K Video Downloader software, click on the **Paste URL** button.
  - Select the quality of the video.
  - Click **Download**
  - The video will begin downloading.
  - To find the video, right click and select Show in Finder on Mac or Show File Location on PC.
